Social Media Do’s and Don’ts :

 Do’s :

Use privacy settings to control who can view your posts and personal information.

Share positive, informative, and meaningful content.

Think carefully before posting anything, as it stays online for long time.

Use strong and unique passwords to keep your accounts safe.

Be respectful and polite while interacting with others online.

Keep your profile professional, especially for academic and career purposes.


Don’ts:

Do not share personal details like phone number, address, or bank information.

Avoid posting offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content.

Do not click on unknown links or messages from strangers.

Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information.

Do not share OTPs, passwords, or confidential data with anyone.

Do not engage in cyberbullying or negative online behavior.
