Stay Safe Online Checklist :
Always check the sender email or message properly before trusting it
Do not click on any suspicious links or unknown attachments
Never share your OTP, passwords or bank details with anyone
Try to use strong passwords and dont keep same password for all accounts
Turn on two factor authentication if possible for extra security
Always check website link before entering any personal information


UPI / Financial Safety:
Never accept unknown payment requests on UPI apps it can be fraud
Do not scan QR codes from unknown people or sources


Report Cybercrime:
Report on cybercrime.gov.in if you face any issue
You can also call helpline number 1930 for help
